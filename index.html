Let's play some Blackjack!

Rules and instructions

The objective in Blackjack is for the player to beat the dealer with a hand that's as close to 21 points as possible without going over. The player or dealer busts if they go over 21 points.

Each card in the hand is worth as many points as the number on it (e.g. 4â™£ = 4 points). Face cards are each worth 10 points and Aces are worth 11 points, unless the total of all other cards in the hand is greater than 10. Then Aces are worth 1 point.

How to play:

  1. Enter your name in the input box below to change the player's name. Otherwise hit 'Save' to keep the default of 'Player.'
  2. When prompted, click "Start Game!" below the square with the cards in it.
  3. The player (you) and the dealer will each be dealt two cards from a shuffled deck. The player's cards will both be face up, while one of the dealer's cards will be hidden.
  4. Decide if you'd like to "Hit" or "Stay" with the buttons below. Pressing "Hit" will add one card to your hand. Pressing "Stay" won't add any cards to your hand and play moves to the dealer. Be careful, hit too many times and you may bust.
  5. The Dealer must continue hitting until they have more than 16 points in their hand or they bust.
  6. The game is then scored and whomever has the higher score wins. A game result is displayed below the playing space.
  7. Press the 'Play Again?' button to reset the playing space before pressing 'Start Game!' for a new round.
